Our Process

From Seed to Cup

Every step is intentional. Every detail matters.

01 01 — Source

Grown at Altitude

Direct partnerships with smallholder farmers across five continents, paying meaningful premiums for exceptional lots and building relationships that last generations.

02 02 — Select

Chosen by the Cup

Rigorous cupping sessions evaluate every lot against exacting flavor standards. Only the top 1% of each harvest earns a place in our collection.

03 03 — Roast

Small Batch Craft

Every origin demands a different roast curve. Our roasters work slowly, reading color and aroma to coax out what each bean was born to become.

04 04 — Deliver

48 Hours to Your Door

Shipped within 48 hours of roasting, sealed for freshness. Peak flavor has a narrow window — we make sure you're in it.
