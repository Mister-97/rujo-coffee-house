Shipping Policy

01 Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days
  • Processing does not include weekends or U.S. holidays

02 Shipping Time

  • Standard delivery within the U.S.: 3–7 business days

03 Order Tracking

  • Once your order ships, you will receive an email with your tracking number
  • Please allow 24–48 hours for the tracking link to update

04 Address Accuracy

  • Customers are responsible for providing a correct and complete shipping address
  • Rujo Coffee House is not liable for delays or lost packages due to incorrect addresses

05 Carrier Delays

  • We are not responsible for delays caused by the postal carrier, weather conditions, or other circumstances outside our control

06 Lost or Stolen Packages

  • If a package is marked delivered but not received, please contact the carrier directly
  • We will assist when possible, but cannot guarantee replacement for packages marked as delivered

Questions? Email us at support@rujocoffeehouse.com
